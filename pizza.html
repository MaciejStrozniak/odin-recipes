Recipe for pizza

Recipe description

Salami pizza
Medium sized

Ingredients

  • Salami
  • Tomato sauce
  • Spices
  • Pizza dough

Steps

  1. Buy best salami in the world
  2. Buy best tomato sauce in the world
  3. Put sauce on the pizza dough
  4. Put salami on pizza dough with sauce over it
  5. Put pizza in the oven
  6. Bake it for around 30 minutes
